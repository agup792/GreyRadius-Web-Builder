## PAGE PURPOSE

This is a free business diagnostic tool that helps visitors 
identify which GreyRadius services are most relevant to their 
current business situation. It is a 4-step quiz with a lead 
capture gate before results are shown.

Add a prominent entry point on the homepage (index.html) 
in two places:
1. In the hero section — add a secondary CTA button next to 
   the existing "Start a conversation" button, labelled:
   "Get a free business diagnostic →"
   linking to business-diagnostic.html

2. In the services section — add a line below the services 
   grid:
   "Not sure which service fits your situation? 
   Take our free 2-minute diagnostic."
   with a text link to business-diagnostic.html

## PAGE STRUCTURE

The page has 6 screens rendered as a single HTML page 
with JavaScript showing/hiding sections. No page reloads.

Screen 1: Welcome screen
Screen 2: Question 1
Screen 3: Question 2
Screen 4: Question 3
Screen 5: Lead capture (name + email)
Screen 6: Results

A progress bar runs across the top of screens 2-5, 
showing steps 1 of 4, 2 of 4, 3 of 4, 4 of 4.
Progress bar uses orange #E8612C fill on navy track.

## RECOMMENDATION LOGIC (if-then rules)

Use a JavaScript function called getRecommendations(q1, q2, 
q3_market, q3_timeline) that returns an array of 2-3 
service objects. Each service object has:
{ name, tagline, description, link, urgency }

SERVICE DEFINITIONS (use these exact names and links):
1. Opportunity Assessment
   tagline: "Validate before you invest"
   link: /services/opportunity-assessment.html

2. Feasibility and TEV
   tagline: "Know if it will work before you build it"
   link: /services/feasibility-and-tev.html

3. Market Entry Execution
   tagline: "Enter new markets with a clear roadmap"
   link: /services/market-entry-execution.html

4. GTM Execution as a Service
   tagline: "Launch and grow with an embedded GTM team"
   link: /services/gtm-execution-as-a-service.html

5. Pitch Book and Fundraising
   tagline: "Raise with confidence"
   link: /services/pitchbook-and-fundraising.html

6. AI Consulting and Transformation
   tagline: "Deliver faster and smarter with AI"
   link: /services/ai-consulting-and-transformation.html

RULES:

Rule Group 1 — Q2 = "Raise funds or prepare for investment":
  Primary: Pitch Book and Fundraising
  Secondary: Opportunity Assessment (if Q1 = A or B)
  Secondary: Feasibility and TEV (if Q1 = C or D)

Rule Group 2 — Q2 = "Enter a new market or geography":
  Primary: Market Entry Execution
  Secondary: Opportunity Assessment
  Tertiary: GTM Execution as a Service 
  (if Q3_timeline = Immediate or Near term)

Rule Group 3 — Q2 = "Evaluate a new business idea":
  Primary: Opportunity Assessment
  Secondary: Feasibility and TEV
  Tertiary: Pitch Book and Fundraising 
  (if Q1 = D Investment stage)

Rule Group 4 — Q2 = "Launch a new product or business":
  Primary: GTM Execution as a Service
  Secondary: Market Entry Execution 
  (if Q3_market != India only)
  Secondary: Opportunity Assessment 
  (if Q1 = A Early stage)

Rule Group 5 — Q2 = "Grow in my existing market":
  Primary: GTM Execution as a Service
  Secondary: Opportunity Assessment
  Tertiary: AI Consulting and Transformation 
  (if Q3_timeline = Immediate)

Rule Group 6 — Q2 = "Use AI to improve how my business operates":
  Primary: AI Consulting and Transformation
  Secondary: GTM Execution as a Service
  Tertiary: Opportunity Assessment

Rule Group 7 — Q1 = D (Investment stage) AND Q2 != fundraising:
  Always add Pitch Book and Fundraising as a third 
  recommendation regardless of Q2 answer.

Rule Group 8 — Q3_timeline = Immediate:
  Add urgency flag to the primary recommendation.
  Show label: "Recommended for immediate action" 
  in orange above the service card.
