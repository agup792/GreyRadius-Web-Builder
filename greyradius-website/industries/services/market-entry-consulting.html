Our POV · 2026

Market entry consulting

Market entry is the highest-stakes decision a company makes in international expansion – and it is the decision most often made without sufficient primary research, realistic cost modelling, or honest competitive intelligence. GreyRadius is a boutique strategy and growth consulting firm that has delivered market entry mandates across India, Gulf, Southeast Asia, and Africa for technology companies, FMCG brands, healthcare businesses, financial services firms, and industrial companies. Our work is grounded in primary research – 30+ expert interviews per mandate – not recycled secondary reports. Every engagement produces a clear Go/Defer/Kill recommendation backed by the evidence to defend it.

Why now? Market entry windows in India, Gulf, Southeast Asia, and Africa are not permanent – PLI application deadlines close, Vision 2030 programme procurement windows move on, and competitive positions solidify. Companies that delay market entry research lose first-mover advantages that compound for years. The right time to start a market entry engagement is before the competition, not after.

Market Reality

What makes this market hard.

  • Most market entry research relies on secondary data – desk research and analyst reports that are 12–24 months stale and do not reflect current market dynamics.
  • Regulatory timelines are routinely underestimated – FSSAI, CDSCO, IRDAI, and Gulf MOH approvals all take significantly longer than international companies expect without local expertise.
  • Channel partner selection is made without adequate due diligence – the most common market entry failure mode is choosing the wrong distributor or system integrator as first commercial partner.
  • Pricing is translated rather than designed – direct translation of international pricing into emerging markets consistently fails; India, Gulf, and Africa all require market-specific pricing logic.
