The challenges we solve for real estate and infrastructure businesses

New market or geography entry

You are a developer, contractor, or service provider looking to enter a new country or city. You need to understand regulatory pathways, demand dynamics, competitive density, and the right go-to-market approach before committing capital.

Project or investment feasibility

You have a site, a concept, or an investment thesis. You need a rigorous feasibility study covering demand validation, financial modelling, pricing benchmarks, and a clear go/no-go recommendation before committing to execution.

Investor readiness and fundraising

You are preparing to raise capital for a development project or real estate venture. You need a credible pitch book, market-validated assumptions, and a financial model that withstands institutional scrutiny.

GTM for real estate products and services

You provide a product or service to the real estate sector – proptech, construction materials, FM services, smart building solutions – and need a structured go-to-market plan to acquire your first clients or scale in a new market.

Competitive intelligence

You need to understand how competitors are positioning, pricing, and winning deals in your target segment – residential, commercial, industrial, or mixed-use – to sharpen your own strategy.

Opportunity identification

You are evaluating whether a new asset class, geography, or product category is worth pursuing. You need a structured opportunity assessment with TAM/SAM/SOM sizing, buyer intelligence, and a prioritized recommendation.

Frequently asked questions

Does GreyRadius work with real estate developers directly or only with service providers? +

Both. We work with developers, investors, and landowners on feasibility and market entry, and with companies that sell products or services into the real estate sector on GTM strategy and market entry.

What does a real estate feasibility study cover? +

Our feasibility studies cover demand validation through primary buyer research, competitive benchmarking, pricing analysis, regulatory pathway, cost structure, revenue model, and a full financial model with ROI projections. We do not rely on published market reports alone.

How long does a market entry engagement take for a real estate company? +

Typically 6–10 weeks for the full research, strategy, and execution planning phase. Feasibility studies typically take 4–6 weeks.

Do you work on projects outside the Gulf? +

Yes. While the Gulf is a primary focus, we run mandates across Southeast Asia, South Asia, and Europe for clients in the real estate and infrastructure sector.
