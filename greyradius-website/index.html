FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does GreyRadius Consulting do?

GreyRadius is a boutique strategy and growth consulting firm with offices across South Asia, the Gulf, Southeast Asia, and Europe. We help founders, CXOs, and business leaders with opportunity assessment, market entry, GTM execution, feasibility studies, pitch books, and fundraising – grounded in primary research and accelerated by AI.

How is GreyRadius different from a large consulting firm?

Every engagement is run by the senior partner who sold it. We ground every insight in primary research – real interviews with buyers, experts, and channel partners, not database downloads. AI-augmented delivery compresses timelines without reducing quality. We bill on outputs, not hours.

What markets does GreyRadius serve?

We work with clients expanding into the Middle East, Southeast Asia, South Asia, and Europe. Our teams are present in four markets with active mandates across more than twenty countries.

How does GreyRadius use AI in consulting delivery?

We use AI internally across research synthesis, financial modelling, competitive mapping, and outreach automation. This compresses delivery timelines by up to 3x without replacing the primary research and senior judgment that drive real decisions.

What is primary research in consulting?

Primary research means gathering insight directly from the market – through structured interviews, expert panels, CATI surveys, and field research. GreyRadius conducts ~30+ interviews and 200+ surveys per mandate. This is the input that desk research and AI cannot replicate.
