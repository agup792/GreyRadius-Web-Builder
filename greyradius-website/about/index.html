Founded 2017 · Dubai

A boutique consulting firm built for execution.

We didn't set out to build the biggest consulting firm. We set out to build the one our founders, CXOs, and government clients actually wanted to work with – small enough to stay close, deep enough to ship real outcomes.

Our Story

Eight years. 200+ projects. Always boutique.

GreyRadius was founded in 2017 in Dubai by Chirag Bansal and Anshuman Kumar – two consultants who had spent years inside larger firms watching good research get diluted by the distance between the partner and the work. The thesis was simple: what if the partner did the interview, ran the workshop, and made the call?

That thesis hasn't changed. What has changed is the scale of the challenge. In 2026, our clients are navigating Vision 2030 entities, funding rounds in a tighter market, AI transformation programmes with no clear playbook, and market entry in jurisdictions where the rulebook is being rewritten in real time.

Today we're four partners – Chirag, Anshuman, Dhiraj, and Pijush – and a senior team of researchers, analysts, and execution specialists across Delhi, Dubai, Singapore, and Brussels. We've delivered 200+ projects across 25+ industries and four office locations across South Asia, the Gulf, Southeast Asia, and Europe. We deliberately stay small. Small enough that every engagement has a partner's name on it.

In 2026, we repositioned the firm around three pillars and six output-led offerings. Every engagement now starts with primary research, uses AI to compress the timeline, and ends with an execution deliverable the client can use on day one.

Our Values

What we stand for.

Output over advice

We are not satisfied with a polished deck. Every engagement ends with a deliverable the client can act on – not an action plan they have to resource separately.

Primary research over desk research

We interview the market. 80% of every insight comes from a real conversation – with buyers, operators, regulators, or channel partners. Not from databases.

AI-augmented over manual

We use AI to compress timelines. Synthesis, transcription, financial modelling, outreach – AI handles the volume so our partners handle the judgment.

Boutique over scale

We have turned down growth that would have required us to dilute partner involvement. We stay boutique because the quality of output depends on it.
