Why GreyRadius.

Three things you won't get from a Big-4. Three things you won't get from a freelancer or an AI tool. We sit in the middle — and the middle is where the work that matters actually gets done.

The Difference

Before GreyRadius. After GreyRadius.

Three structural challenges most strategy projects carry — and how we fix each one.

Before

Strategy based on generic market data

Reports built on industry databases, desk research, and analyst reports that everyone else has access to. No primary voice from buyers, competitors, or regulators. Insight commoditised before it's applied.

After

Strategy based on primary intelligence

80% of every insight comes from a real conversation — buyer interviews, channel partner calls, expert panels, CATI surveys. The market told us what the database couldn't.

Before

Execution limited to internal team only

The consultant hands off a strategy deck. The client's internal team is expected to execute a market-entry plan, run GTM motions, or close a round — without external support, networks, or bench strength.

After

Execution focused with external market view

Five of six offerings include execution — entity setup, partners signed, pipeline built, round closed. We work alongside the internal team, bringing the external networks and market leverage that make the plan real.

Before

High cost, slow delivery by large firms

A Big-4 TEV for a $50M deal costs $150–300k and takes 12–16 weeks. You pay for brand premium, infrastructure, and an army of analysts you'll never meet. The insight itself is the same.

After

Cost-effective & timely execution

Same-quality answer in 2–6 weeks at boutique pricing. AI-augmented delivery compresses timelines without compromising the human work that matters. Output-billed — you pay when milestones arrive.

vs. The Big Firms

What you won't get from a Big-4.

Three things the big firms structurally cannot deliver — no matter who runs your engagement.

Partner-led delivery — every time

In a Big-4 engagement, the partner sells the work. The work is then delivered by a team of associates and senior consultants. You may see the partner at the kick-off call and the final presentation. At GreyRadius, the partner who sells the work also runs it — the interviews, the workshops, the model reviews, the negotiations. Every time.

Boutique pricing

A Big-4 TEV for a $50M transaction costs $150–300k. The same work at GreyRadius costs $25–80k, takes 4 weeks instead of 12, and comes with primary research the Big-4 wouldn't do. The overhead difference isn't in the talent — it's in the brand premium and the infrastructure supporting it.

Decision-ready answers

Big-4 reports are beautiful, thorough, and defensible. They're built for a different world — where the audience is a board, a regulator, or a fund committee that wants all the data before the decision. Founders and CXOs need a recommendation with a clear answer. We build for that.

vs. Freelancers & AI Tools

What you won't get from a freelancer or an AI tool.

Cheaper doesn't mean better when the deliverable is a decision your business depends on.

Primary research at scale

A freelance analyst does desk research. An AI tool does desk research faster. Neither of them will call 25 buyers in three geographies over two weeks, synthesise what they said, and tell you which of them are real customers and which are just curious. We do.

Multi-disciplinary execution

Market entry requires a researcher, a financial modeller, a legal navigator, a recruiter, and a network of local partners. A freelancer specialises. We bring the whole capability set, coordinated by a partner who's done it before in the same geography.

Accountability you can put on a P&L

We bill on outputs, not hours. That means if the output doesn't arrive, you don't pay the milestone. No freelancer, no AI tool, and no Big-4 firm bills this way. We do because we believe in the work.
