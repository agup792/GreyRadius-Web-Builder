The Partners

Four partners.
One method.

Every engagement has a partner's name on it. Not a team number. A person you can call.

Chirag Bansal

Co-founder & Managing Partner

Chirag founded GreyRadius in 2017 after a decade in strategy consulting across India and the GCC. He leads market entry, GTM execution, and government advisory mandates. His primary research programme has generated 15,000+ interviews across 25 industries.

Chirag is based in Dubai and personally leads every GCC-market engagement.

Dubai, UAE

Anshuman Kumar

Co-founder & Partner, India & SEA

Anshuman co-founded GreyRadius in 2017 and built the Delhi practice from the ground up. He leads India strategy and SME advisory, family-business transformation, and Southeast Asia expansion engagements from our Singapore office.

Anshuman splits his time between Delhi and Singapore, depending on the mandate.

Delhi, India · Singapore

Dhiraj

Partner, Fundraising & Capital Advisory

Dhiraj leads GreyRadius's pitchbook and fundraising practice. In the past three years, he has personally overseen 540+ investor interviews across Series A and B mandates, and supported 12 rounds from deck to close. His track record includes a USD 500M IPO-stage engagement for an edtech company.

Dhiraj is based in Delhi and takes selected mandates in Dubai and Singapore.

Delhi, India

Pijush Sinha

Partner, Growth & AI Practice

Pijush leads GreyRadius's GTM execution and AI consulting practices. He brings deep experience in B2B SaaS growth, AI product strategy, and go-to-market execution for technology companies entering India and the GCC. He joined as partner in 2022.

Pijush is based in Dubai and leads all AI transformation and GTM-XaaS mandates.

Dubai, UAE
