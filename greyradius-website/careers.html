Join GreyRadius

Build your consulting career at GreyRadius.

Not always. But when we do, we're looking for a specific kind of person – and we'd rather they find us than the other way around.

What we're looking for.

If you're a senior consultant with 5+ years in strategy, market research, or fundraising advisory – and you want to work in a boutique where you're close to the founder, close to the research, and close to the outcome – we'd like to hear from you.

We're not looking for generalists or people who need large teams to get work done. We're looking for people who are already doing primary research, already running engagements independently, and who are frustrated that the firms they work for won't let them do the work the right way.

We're a team of four partners and a small senior team. Every person we add needs to raise the average, not just fill a headcount.

You should have

  • ✓ 5+ years in strategy consulting, market research, or deal advisory
  • ✓ Experience running primary research independently
  • ✓ Track record of partner-level client exposure
  • ✓ Deep familiarity with India, GCC, or SEA markets
  • ✓ Comfort working across time zones and geographies

You should want

  • ✓ Direct client exposure from day one
  • ✓ Output-billed work with clear accountability
  • ✓ A firm small enough that your name is on the work
  • ✓ Freedom to use AI tools to do the work faster and better

Send us a note.

Email careers@greyradius.com with a short note about yourself, the kind of work you want to do, and why GreyRadius. No CV required at this stage – we'll ask for it if there's something here.

What kind of work does GreyRadius do?

Market entry, GTM execution, opportunity assessment, feasibility studies, pitch books, and fundraising advisory – across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, South Asia, and Europe. Every engagement is primary-research-led and execution-focused.

What experience level does GreyRadius hire?

We hire consultants with 5+ years of experience in strategy, market research, or advisory roles. We value primary research experience, client-facing delivery, and comfort with ambiguity.
