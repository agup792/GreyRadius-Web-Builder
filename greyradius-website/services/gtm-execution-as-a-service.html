Common questions.

Do we need a sales team for this to work?

No. We can run the entire top-of-funnel with no internal sales team. When qualified meetings are being generated, you need a founder or someone senior to take the conversation forward. We don't close deals for you — but we get you into the right rooms.

What's the difference between GTM-XaaS and hiring a fractional CMO?

A fractional CMO advises on strategy and oversees a team. We run the operation — the sequences, the targeting, the message testing, the pipeline reporting. We also bring primary research from buyer interviews that a CMO typically doesn't do. And we're accountable to outcomes, not just strategy.

How do you define the outcome metrics?

In the scoping call, we agree on three metrics: pipeline meeting rate (qualified meetings per week), pipeline value at 90 days, and revenue generated during the engagement period. These are defined before we start, written into the engagement letter, and reviewed weekly.

Ready to have us run your pipeline?

Tell us your ICP and your current state. We'll tell you what 90 days looks like.
