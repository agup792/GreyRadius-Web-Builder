Common questions.

How is this different from desk research we could do internally?

Desk research tells you what's already published. Our research tells you what buyers actually think, what channel partners will say yes to, and what regulators are about to do. These are three very different things, and the gap between them is where most market-entry failures happen.

Can we get the interview transcripts?

Yes. All interviews conducted with interviewee consent are delivered in full. We use Otter.ai for transcription. Sensitive interviews may be lightly redacted by agreement, but the source is always identified by role, company type, and date.

What if the answer is "Kill"?

We deliver the Kill recommendation. We've done this 40+ times. The single biggest value of an Opportunity Assessment is having the evidence to say no — without having committed 6 months and $500k to find out the market wasn't there.

How do you find the interviewees?

Combination of our own network (built over 200+ projects), expert platforms (AlphaSense, Tegus, GLG), and outbound outreach via Apollo + Clay. We never rely on a single source — source diversity is a quality signal.

Can this feed into a Feasibility & TEV engagement?

Yes — and this is the most common path. Opportunity Assessment → "Go" → Feasibility & TEV. The interviews and model from the Assessment feed directly into the TEV, reducing the overall cost by roughly 30%.
