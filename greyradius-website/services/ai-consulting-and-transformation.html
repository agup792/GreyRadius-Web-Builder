Common questions.

Do we need a data team before we can engage?

No. The AI Opportunity Assessment is explicitly designed for companies that don't yet know what data they have or what AI can do with it. We do the inventory as part of the engagement.

Which AI tools do you work with?

We are tool-agnostic. We use Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and others internally, and have experience deploying OpenAI APIs, Anthropic APIs, Google Vertex, and Azure OpenAI at client sites. We recommend the tool that fits the use case and the client's infrastructure – not the one we have a commercial relationship with.

What if the pilot doesn't work?

Pilots fail. That's why they're pilots. A failed pilot with clear learnings is a success – it tells you which direction not to invest $2M in. We define "success" for a pilot as: the hypothesis was tested, the result is clear, and the next decision is made. Not: the AI worked perfectly.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is AI consulting for businesses?

Identifying where AI tools and automation can be embedded into existing workflows to reduce delivery time, lower cost, and improve output quality – without replacing the human judgment that drives real decisions.

How does GreyRadius use AI in its own delivery?

We use AI internally across research synthesis, financial modelling, competitive mapping, and outreach automation. This is how we deliver 3x faster at boutique pricing.

Is AI consulting relevant for mid-sized businesses?

Yes. Most AI efficiency gains are available without large tech teams – through off-the-shelf tools configured correctly for specific workflows and decision points.

What is AI-augmented consulting?

A delivery model where AI handles repeatable analytical tasks – data synthesis, model building, competitive mapping – while senior consultants focus on primary research, client judgment, and execution. The result is faster delivery at lower cost without sacrificing insight quality.
